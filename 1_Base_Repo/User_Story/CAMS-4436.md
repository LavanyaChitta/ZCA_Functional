CAMS-4436 - Claims tab- Exposure amount to be populated for Canada, USA and rest of world locations for Casualty
Requirement:

For Casualty- If the exposure base is Turnover or Revenue, then automatically pick the turnover / revenue

For multiple NAICS, Exposure amount = Sum of Exposure values (to be calculated for Canada, USA and Rest of world)

If exposure is not revenue or turnover, then UW/UA would need to update the exposure in the UI
