Companent Middle Market

Requirement:

Content of the newer version of the Form ZC3676 0325a should not have the highlighted lines as per the attachment. Other content should remain the same.

Newer version of the form ZC 3676 0325a should be inferred whenever the exclusion ZC3676 is selected on UMB.

Older version of the form should be retired and should not infer after the introduction of the new version.

Acceptance Criteria 1:

As a system user

when I add ZC 3676 exclusion in my policy and preferred language is English

then I want the system to infer ZC3676U 0325a form during binding process.

Acceptance Criteria 2:

As a system user

when I add ZC 3676 exclusion in my policy and preferred language is French

then I want the system to infer ZC3676G 0325a form during binding process.

Acceptance Criteria 3:

As a system user

when I add ZC 3676 exclusion in my policy

then I do not want the system to infer ZC3676 0325 form during binding process.

Acceptance Criteria 4:

As a system user

when I add ZC 3676 exclusion in my policy and validate ZC3676 0325a form

then I want the system to generate the content form as per attached mock up.
